Freight Forwarding

Streamlined Logistics for Global & Domestic Shipping

At Etash, our Freight Forwarding Services provide businesses with seamless, cost-effective, and efficient cargo transportation solutions, ensuring the smooth movement of goods across domestic and international routes. With expertise in multimodal logistics, we facilitate freight movement via road, air, rail, and sea, offering end-to-end solutions for exporters, importers, and supply chain partners. Our strong presence across Northeast India and North West Bengal, along with a vast network of logistics partners, allows us to manage shipments efficiently, even in challenging terrains and remote locations.

Leveraging advanced tracking technology, customs clearance expertise, and optimized route planning, we ensure that freight is transported with speed, accuracy, and compliance. From handling bulk cargo and commercial shipments to time-sensitive consignments, Etash provides customized logistics solutions tailored to industry-specific needs. As part of our growth strategy, we are continuously expanding our freight forwarding operations, aiming to connect businesses to key national and global trade hubs with seamless supply chain integration.

With a commitment to reliability, transparency, and operational excellence, Etash Freight Forwarding simplifies logistics, allowing businesses to focus on growth while we handle the complexities of cargo movement.
