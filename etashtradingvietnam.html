Etash Delivery Import Export Company Ltd

Bringing Vietnam's Finest Teas to the World

At Etash Delivery Import Export Company Ltd, we are proud to introduce the rich heritage of Vietnamese tea to the global market. In collaboration with Phuong Xa Agricultural Product Processing Private Enterprise, we are dedicated manufacturers and exporters of the finest green, black, and specialty teas that Vietnam has to offer.

From the vibrant freshness of our green teas to the bold richness of our black teas, and the distinctive nuances of our specialty blends, we curate an authentic taste of Vietnam in every sip. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and fair trade ensures that each batch meets the highest standards, reflecting the passion and craftsmanship of Vietnamese tea farmers.

🌿 Sustainably Sourced, Ethically Delivered

We work closely with local farmers, ensuring fair pricing practices and promoting environmentally responsible cultivation methods. By prioritizing sustainability and ethical sourcing, we not only deliver superior tea but also support the growth and well-being of rural farming communities.

🌎 Expanding Global Reach with Trusted Partnerships

In a remarkably short span, Etash has earned the trust and loyalty of international buyers, thanks to our unwavering commitment to excellence and reliability. We are currently exporting to 8 countries namely India, Russia, Kazakhstan, Pakistan, Afghanistan, Turkey, Spain and China.

  • Redefining Trade with Taste & Trust
  • Cultivating Flavor, Exporting Quality
  • Blending Tradition with Global Expansion
  • Seamlessly Shipping Vietnam's Finest Globally

Our reputation for honesty, transparency, and delivering premium-quality products has made us a preferred supplier in the global tea market.
